BallBoy Plus

It was a beautiful sunny day. BallBoy and his friend BallGirl enjoyed singing in the garden, when suddenly the sky darkened and strong noise filled the sky. Evil cubes suddenly appeared from neighbour village and kidnapped BallGirl. BallBoy was completely hopeless.

BallBoy has to defeat evil cubes and save BallGirl. So help him on his mission.

You can defeat evil cubes if the ball has the same color as evil cube when they collide. Touch/press left part of screen for moving the character(ball) left. Touch/press right part of screen for moving the character right. Cubes have four colors(blue, red, green and yellow). The game has 45 awesome and well-designed levels.
